Is chat GPT a threat to developers?

ChatGPT is a state-of-the-art language model developed by OpenAI that is trained to generate human-like text. It is based on the GPT (Generative Pre-trained Transformer) architecture and is fine-tuned on a massive amount of conversational data. We should consider it as a new technology only and use it wisely instead od being worried.
